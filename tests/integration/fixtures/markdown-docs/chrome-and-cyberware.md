# Chrome and Cyberware

In Night City, flesh is weakness. Everyone with enough eddies gets chromed — cyberware implants that push the human body past its biological limits.

## Cyberpsychosis

Too much chrome and you lose yourself. Cyberpsychosis is the term for when a person's humanity erodes under the weight of excessive augmentation. MaxTac — Night City's elite cyberpsycho response unit — are themselves heavily augmented, fighting fire with fire.

Ripperdocs like Viktor Vektor walk the line between healer and arms dealer. A good ripperdoc knows when to say no — when the next implant might push a client over the edge into cyberpsychosis.

## Sandevistan and Kerenzikov

The Sandevistan is a military-grade operating system implant that dramatically accelerates the user's perception of time. David Martinez famously used a Sandevistan beyond its rated limits, burning through his nervous system in the process.

The Kerenzikov is a more subtle reflex booster — it activates on dodge, slowing perceived time just enough to evade incoming fire. Most mercs in Night City run one or the other, never both.
